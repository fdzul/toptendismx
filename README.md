# toptendismx

<!-- badges: start -->

<!-- badges: end -->

The goal of toptendismx is to provide morbidity and mortality databases
for the ten most prevalent diseases in Mexico. The spatial resolution is
municipal, and the temporal resolution is annual. The datasets were
obtained from the Ministry of Health, General Directorate of Health
Information, through Dynamic Cubes. We selected the corresponding ICD-10
codes.
